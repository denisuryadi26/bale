:: Qod anshoha lii abi warobats bihusniha
Umi wa arsyadanil ustadz wa mualimi 2x
• Lakin faqod ahmaltu nusha wa irsyada
Walam akum musgian rusdam min ayifami
Wa anzaltu likulli hububa rih masala
Taruhu bil adzani tagniyyatan nasimi
• Fakuntu mustagian bidausyatiddunya
Wasuhirtu bidzuhuri anwail hutomi
Waaknudu ni’mataan mirobbina kibro
Waangsaitu solatan kadza walam asumi
• Falammastas artu birro’si mamlu syaiban
Walajladu gudunuw wal a’zumu rimami
Tanadamtu wadumu ul aini salat dama
Afaqo mu’simun min gomal goyyi zulami
• Fakhosirul ubbadi attaka multazian
Murtazian rohmatan mirobbihi rohimi
Fahablana taubatan wa’fu annal khotoya
Wagfirlana mamado yawasial karomi
