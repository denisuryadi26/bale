Assubhubada

:: Assubhubada min tol’atihi, wal lailu daja miw warfarotihi
• Faqor rusula fadlan wa ala, wa hada subulan bidi lalatihi
Kanzul karomi maulan niami, hadil umami bisyari atihi
• Azkan nasabi a’lal hasabi, kullah arobi fi khid matihi
Sa’tissyajaru nataqol hajaru, syuqqol qomaru bi’isyarotihi
• Jibrillu ata lailal isro, warrobbu da’ah lihadrotihi
Nalas syarofa wallahu afa, amma salafa min ummatihi
