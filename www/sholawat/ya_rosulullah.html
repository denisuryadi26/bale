Atfatayaji rottal alami ya uhailal judi wal karomi
:: Nahnnu jironum bidzilharomi haromil ikhsani wal Hasani

:: Wabi ayatil qur’ani unu fattaid fina akhol wahani

:: Walanal ma’la wakhifu minna fa’laman hadza wakun wakuni
Walana khoirul anami abu wa ‘aliyul murtado hasabu
