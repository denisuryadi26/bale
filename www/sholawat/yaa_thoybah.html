Yaa Thoybah

Yaa thoybah yaa dzawal ayana Istagnalik wal hawa nadana
• Sayidi yaa abba bakri, hubbukum fill qiyami dzukhri
Yaa Umar iqdihi bi umri, wa kadza sayidi usmani
• Shohibull qob’dotil asliah wa dzawil bahzati saniah
Walahu rutbatul aliah man ilaihi babur royani
• Yaa ali yab’na abi tholib min kumul masdarul mawahid
Halturo hal uro lihajib indakum afdolul gilmana
• Asiyadal asan wal husaini illah nabi qurrotu aini
Waya syababal jannataini haddukum shohibull furqona
